Magical come back

In 2022, Rafael Nadal has achieved the impossible. After a long period of injury due to illness, Rafael Nadal returned in January for the Australian Open, which he had only won once before in 2009. After an exceptional tournament, Nadal found himself in the final against world number 2 Medvedev. He led by 2 sets but after 4 hours and 35 minutes of play, Nadal made an incredible comeback winning 2-6 / 6-7 / 6-4 / 6-4 / 7-5. Then, after 14 consecutive wins, another injury ended his comeback. But at 35, Nadal went throughout to reach 22 grand slam victories by winning the French Open in style 6-3 / 6-3 / 6-0 against Ruud in the final. After almost 6 months of injury in the last 9 months, Rafael Nadal was for the first time since 2019 world number 1. 2023 started slowly for Rafa but he will be back for Roland Garros 2023 to make history.
